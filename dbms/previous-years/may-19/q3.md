â†’ As the weak entities do not have any primary key, they cannot be identified on their own, so they depend on some other entity (known as owner entity).

â†’ The weak entities have total participation constraint (existence dependency) in its identifying relationship with owner identity. Weak entity types have partial keys. Partial Keys are set of attributes with the help of which the tuples of the weak entities can be distinguished and identified.
